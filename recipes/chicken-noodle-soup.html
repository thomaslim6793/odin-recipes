Chicken noodle soup

Description

Nothing better than a good ol' chicken noodle soup for when you are
out of energy! If there is a textbook for comfort foods, this meal would
be right on the front page!
The total time to make is 40 minutes and serves 6 people.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • ½ cup chopped onion
  • ½ cup chopped celery
  • 4 (14.5 ounce) cans chicken broth
  • 1 (14.5 ounce) can vegetable broth
  • ½ pound chopped cooked chicken breast
  • 1 ½ cups egg noodles
  • 1 cup sliced carrots
  • ½ teaspoon dried basil
  • ½ teaspoon dried oregano
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Melt butter in a large pot over medium heat. Add onion and celery and cook until just tender, about 5 minutes.
  2. Add chicken broth, vegetable broth, chicken, egg noodles, carrots, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper.
    Stir to combine and bring to a boil.
  3. Reduce heat and simmer for 20 minutes.
